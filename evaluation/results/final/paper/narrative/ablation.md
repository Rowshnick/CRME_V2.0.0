# Ablation Study

The ablation analysis evaluated the contribution of the major CRME architectural components by measuring aggregate performance degradation after component removal.

The largest aggregate degradation was observed after removing the decisions component, with a degradation score of 52.08.

The component ranking indicates that decision management and relational knowledge structures make substantial contributions to the evaluated CRME performance profile.

The results should be interpreted as comparative ablation evidence rather than as independent causal estimates of component importance.
