# Results

The CRME evaluation pipeline was executed across 2 datasets and 5 evaluation metrics.

The comparative evaluation identified SRCB_v1 as the best-performing dataset across all reported metrics.

For MCS, the system achieved a score of 47.5 on SRCB_v1.
For SRS, the system achieved a score of 100.0 on SRCB_v1.
For KGQ, the system achieved a score of 40.0 on SRCB_v1.
For RRS, the system achieved a score of 43.33 on SRCB_v1.
For CES, the system achieved a score of 57.71 on SRCB_v1.

These results provide the baseline performance profile used for the subsequent component ablation analysis.
